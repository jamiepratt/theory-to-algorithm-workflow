The construct

Say exactly what the number means.

The estimand is receptive knowledge of individual target-language surface forms inside a fixed, versioned pool.

Recommended result label
“Estimated receptive knowledge: 3,850 surface forms from our Pre‑A1–B1 pool.”

Not “words known in the language.” Not lemmas. Not productive vocabulary.

CEFR is language-independent; language-specific form inventories require separate operational definitions. Council of Europe, Reference Level Descriptions.

Bayesian updating

Yes—this is Bayes’ theorem.

For stratum h, pₕ is the unknown proportion of forms this learner knows.

Posterior is the correct name for the updated distribution after seeing responses.
Bayes’ theorem in model form
posterior ∝ likelihood × prior
Correct
α ← α + 1

Evidence pushes probability upward.

Don’t know
β ← β + 1

Evidence pushes probability downward.

prior: pₕ ~ Beta(1,1)
likelihood: pₕᵏ (1−pₕ)ⁿ⁻ᵏ
posterior: pₕ | data ~ Beta(1+k, 1+n−k)

Future calibration

The calibration unit is the complete item—not merely the form.

Identity

Immutable item version

Form + intended sense/context + translation + distractors + language pair.

Exposure

Balanced randomized queues

Cycle through items within every stratum; never repeat an item for one learner.

Response

Keep three outcomes

Correct, wrong, and Don’t know—even if stage-one scoring temporarily collapses the last two.

Position

Record order and timing

Question position, response time, option order, device, and voluntary stopping.

Linking

Preserve anchors

Cumulative level pools naturally connect calibration scales from Pre‑A1 through C1.

Selection

Log inclusion probability

Preserves valid weighting if bank-exposure controls later alter pure random sampling.

Upgrade path

Start transparent. Earn complexity with data.

Now
Stratified Beta–binomial

Balanced sampling, honest uncertainty, no calibrated item parameters required.

After pilot
Partial pooling

Borrow strength across adjacent ranks after coverage simulations show it remains calibrated.

After calibration
IRT + adaptive testing

Use estimated item difficulty and separate correct, wrong, and Don’t-know response behaviour.

Recommended launch specification

A defensible estimate now. A calibratable bank later.
